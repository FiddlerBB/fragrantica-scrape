Terror & Magnificence by BeauFort London is a fragrance for women and men. Terror & Magnificence was launched in 2019. The nose behind this fragrance is Pia Long. Top notes are Birch Tar, Black Pepper and Saffron; middle notes are Incense, Tobacco and Papyrus; base notes are Haitian Vetiver, Myrrh, Labdanum, Benzoin and Pebbles.
